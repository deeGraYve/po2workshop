Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the PO2 Workshop?

The workshop is open to researchers, graduate students, and professionals in oceanography, geology, climate science, and related fields. Registration is required as space is limited.

How can I submit research for presentation?

Abstract submissions open approximately 6 months before each workshop. Please submit your abstract (max 500 words) through our online portal. All submissions are peer-reviewed by our scientific committee.

Are there registration fees?

Yes, registration fees vary based on academic status (student, postdoc, professional). Early bird registration discounts are available. Fee waivers may be granted in special circumstances.

Will presentations be recorded or streamed?

Keynote presentations and selected panels will be live-streamed. All presentations will be recorded with speaker permission and made available to registered participants after the workshop.

How often is the PO2 Workshop held?

The workshop is typically held biennially (every two years) at different institutions around the world. Special focused workshops may be organized in intervening years.
