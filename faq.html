Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this workshop for?

The PO2 Workshop: Notable Hypoxic Events of the Geologic Past is for anyone interested in the topic of hypoxia or anoxia in our past oceans. Discussion will likely focus on events associated with changes in oxygenation such as the Ocean Anoxic Events (OAEs) and the Messinian Salinity Crisis, and other major perturbations such as the PETM. Researchers from all career stages and subdisciplines are welcome.

What is the workshop format?

The workshop with consist of presentations from invited speakers, poster sessions, and group discussions. Our goals is to synthesis knowledge on the topic. The workshop will be hybrid, allowing for both in-person and virtual participation, including virtual poster options.

How do I register for the workshop?

Registration will open in summer of 2025. Please watch this space or sign up for the PO2 Newsletter here for updates.

Is there a student discount?

Yes! We will be offering reduced rates for Early Career Researchers and those attending online.
