Bristol, UK - November 2024

Ice sheets, ocean oxygenation and the carbon cycle: Constraining changes in seawater oxygenation during the late Quaternary

This workshop explored global ocean oxygenation and oxygen distribution on glacial-interglacial timescales during the late Quaternary, including a critical assessment and comparison of different proxy and model approaches. A synthesis product following the workshop is currently in progress.
