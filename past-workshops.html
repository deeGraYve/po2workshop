Past Workshops

Explore previous editions of the Past Ocean Oxygen Workshop, featuring research presentations, keynote speeches, and scientific breakthroughs in oceanic oxygen studies.

PO2 2024 - Sydney, Australia

University of Sydney, Marine Research Center

Focused on Pacific Ocean oxygen minimum zones and their response to climate change over the past millennium.

Highlights:

  • Keynote by Dr. Emma Williams on "Deep Pacific Oxygen Depletion Rates: 1000-2000 CE"
  • Panel discussion on sediment core analysis techniques
  • Introduction of new paleoceanographic proxies for oxygen reconstruction

PO2 2022 - Bergen, Norway

Norwegian Institute for Marine Research

Examined North Atlantic oxygen trends throughout the Holocene with special focus on anthropogenic impacts.

Highlights:

  • Comparison of pre-industrial and modern ocean oxygen distribution
  • Advanced modeling techniques for historical oxygen reconstruction
  • Collaborative project launch: "North Atlantic Oxygen Timeline"
