Getting There

By Air

The nearest airport is San Diego International Airport (SAN), approximately 20 miles from the venue. Direct flights are available from major cities worldwide.

By Public Transportation

From the airport, take the MTS Bus Route 992 to Downtown, then transfer to the Blue Line Trolley to Old Town Transit Center. From there, bus routes 30 or 105 will take you within walking distance of the venue.

By Car

From Interstate 5, take the La Jolla Village Drive exit and head west. Turn left onto North Torrey Pines Road, then right onto Coastal Drive. Parking is available at the venue for $15/day.

Accommodation

We have negotiated special rates at the following hotels for workshop attendees:

  • Coastal View Hotel - 0.3 miles from venue, $180/night with code "PO2WORKSHOP"
  • Oceanside Suites - 0.7 miles from venue, $150/night with code "PO2WORKSHOP"
  • Marine Research Lodge - On campus, $120/night for students, $140/night for faculty

Please book early as availability is limited, especially for on-campus accommodation.
